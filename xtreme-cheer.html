About the Team

Flippers Xtreme Cheer is a year-round competitive cheer program running May to May. We hold athletes to a high attendance standard and a positive-atmosphere standard. Coaches, athletes, and parents all show up for each other. This team competes hard and cheers louder.

No prior cheer experience is required. Just bring commitment, a good attitude, and a willingness to work hard. If that sounds like your daughter, this is her team.
