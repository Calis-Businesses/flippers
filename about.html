We made a choice a long time ago: we would be the gym that says yes.

Our Story

Flippers Gymnastics and Cheer has been part of this community because we made a deliberate choice: we would be the gym that says yes. Yes to the child who has never done a backbend. Yes to the family watching every dollar. Yes to the kid who needs a structured, positive space where adults hold them to a real standard.

Our facility is functional, not flashy. Our equipment gets the job done. What we invest in is coaching, atmosphere, and the belief that every kid can surprise themselves when they are given the right environment.

What We Believe

Every child belongs here

No prior experience required. All abilities welcomed. We have held everyone to a real standard of discipline and respect while keeping the atmosphere warm and encouraging since we opened.

Discipline with heart

We do not tolerate disrespect, excessive absences, or cutting corners. We hold parents to the same standard. When everyone shows up, the whole team rises.

Accessible excellence

We have kept our prices low on purpose. Great gymnastics and cheer coaching should not be reserved for families who can afford elite facilities. It is for every kid on every block.

Whole-child development

We teach more than flips in here. We teach showing up, working hard, and being kind. Those are the things that stick long after the season ends.

Our Coaches

Our coaches are experienced, passionate, and firm, because they know that structure is a gift. Each coach brings years of experience in gymnastics, cheer, dance, or martial arts and a genuine belief that every kid has potential.
